Our expertise is unique and recognized around the world: the provision of essential ingredients for perfumes and fragrances, cosmetics and essential oils. Our FranceAlcools® brand is particularly well-known among fine fragrance players for whom excellence is always the priority.

Thanks to our experience in the production and commercialization of premium natural or denatured alcohols we can offer a wide range of formulations. For example, CosmEthyl® alcohols are frequently requested formulations for cosmetic applications.

And in the manufacture of natural and authentic cosmetics such as scrubs, depilatory creams and lipsticks, amongst others, our plant-derived sugar and Stevia products offer renowned solutions.
